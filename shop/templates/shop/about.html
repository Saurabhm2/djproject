StudyAreaLive Cart!

StudyArea Live Pvt Ltd. is an Indian educational platform based in Pune, India. Founded by Saurabh in 2024, the company initially focused on providing free PDF downloads of study materials for students across various disciplines, such as engineering, pharmacy, and computer science. The website later expanded its offerings to include exam preparation guides, agricultural studies, and resources for other bachelor's courses. The service competes primarily with other online educational resource platforms in India. It has carved out a significant presence by catering specifically to the needs of students preparing for competitive exams like the MHT CET and various state board exams. StudyArea Live has become a popular destination for students seeking easily accessible and free academic resources. The platform is particularly dominant in the field of providing educational content for undergraduate courses, and it continues to expand its library of resources to meet the growing demands of students across the country.
